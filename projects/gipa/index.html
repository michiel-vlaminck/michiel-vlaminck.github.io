GIPA

January 1, 2014 1-minute read

The goal of this project is to develop a generic interoperability framework for augmented reality applications. The platform is able to host a variety of applications in the domains of remote collaboration, engineering, smart cities, gaming, entertainment, etc. My task in the project was focused on the modelling and analysis of the environment by means of 3D reconstruction and the detection of 3D geometric primitives.

Computer science engineer & PhD in 3D computer vision with a strong interest in artificial intelligence and autonomous robots.
