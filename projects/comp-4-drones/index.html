COMP4DRONES

July 1, 2019 1-minute read

COMP4DRONES is a European project that aims to provide a framework of key technologies to facilitate safe and autonomous drones (https://www.comp4drones.eu/). It will ease the development of new applications and functionalities on the field of transport, construction, surveillance and inspection, logistics and agriculture.

My work in the project focuses on the use case of inspection of wind turbines. More specifically I aim at detecting degradation in the form of corrosion. To that end, I am using data acquired with imec’s dual hyperspectral camera system.

The project is in collaboration with the HSI team of imec and the company Airobot.

Computer science engineer & PhD in 3D computer vision with a strong interest in artificial intelligence and autonomous robots.
