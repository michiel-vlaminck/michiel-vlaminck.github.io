Courses

Research project

Research Project (ECTS-card) is a course in the Master of Science in Computer Science Engineering. The aim of the course is to teach the students elementary elements of scientific research, and especially on the process of searching the literature and scientific writing. I assist one of the topics on “Hyperspectral image classification”.

Computer graphics

The course computer graphics (ECTS-card), lectured by prof. Aleksandra Pizurica, is taught in the Master of Science in Computer Science Engineering. I take care of the projects of the course, which consist of a programming assignment (using OpenGL or VTK) and focus on a particular subtopic of computer graphics. Among the topics are 3D scene generation, 3D space partitioning and mesh simplification.

Information technology and data processing

The course information technology and data processing (ECTS-card), lectured by prof. Sofie Verbrugge and dr. Jan Aelterman, is taught in the Master of Science in Industrial Engineering and Operations Research. I assist the practical exercise sessions, where students are taught on how to query a relational database by means of the SQL query language.

Computer science engineer & PhD in 3D computer vision with a strong interest in artificial intelligence and autonomous robots.
